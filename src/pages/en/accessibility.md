**Last Updated: March 30, 2026**

Fractal Computers considers accessibility a core value. We have designed this website to meet the WCAG 2.1 AA accessibility standards.

## Accessibility Widget Features
Our site features a dedicated accessibility menu allowing for a personalized browsing experience. The widget supports: text resizing, contrast adjustments, spacing modifications, an enlarged cursor, a visual reading guide, Text-to-Speech (TTS), and Speech-to-Text (STT) capabilities.

## Compatibility and Assistive Technologies
The website is highly optimized for modern internet browsers and provides full support for assistive technologies, including leading screen readers like NVDA and JAWS, as well as comprehensive keyboard navigation.

## Physical Office Accessibility (8 Masada St., Bney Brak)
Our main office is located at 8 Masada St., Bney Brak. The building is equipped with an elevator, and the entrance is partially accessible. **Please be advised: There are NO designated accessible restrooms on site.** Visitors requiring special accommodations should contact us in advance to coordinate their arrival.
