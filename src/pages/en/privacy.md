**Last Updated:** March 26, 2026

Fractal Computers and Communications 2002 Ltd ("Fractal" or "we") respects your privacy. This policy outlines how we collect, store, and use personal information in accordance with the provisions of the Israeli Privacy Protection Law (1981) and its Amendment 13.

## 1. Information Collection and Purpose
Using this website does not require registration. However, when filling out the "Contact Us" form, we will ask you to provide basic identifying details (name, email, phone, and inquiry details).

You are under no legal obligation to provide this information; it is provided entirely of your own free will and consent. The information is collected solely for the purpose of contacting you, answering your questions, evaluating the suitability of our services to your needs, and managing your inquiry.

## 2. Cross-Border Data Transfer, Third Parties & Infrastructure
We explicitly commit not to sell, trade, or transfer your personal information to any third parties for commercial purposes. 
Our technical infrastructure is hosted and secured by Cloudflare. Cloudflare infrastructure automatically processes network traffic data, including IP addresses, strictly for cybersecurity (WAF), routing, and site reliability purposes. Furthermore, business inquiries are managed through our corporate communication systems (e.g., Microsoft Office 365).
Consequently, data may be transferred to and securely stored on servers utilizing global cloud networks outside the State of Israel. These transfers are strictly governed by applicable laws (including GDPR frameworks applicable to global cloud providers) and adhere to rigorous international security standards.

## 3. Cookies, Tracking & Essential Storage
Safeguarding your privacy is a core value. This website is built on a "Privacy by Design" framework, meaning that by default, **the site employs a NO TRACKING policy**.
Third-party analytics, personalized advertising, and conversion tracking tools (such as Google Analytics and Google Ads, which provide personalized marketing and store data like IP addresses and visited pages on servers in the US/EU) are **strictly blocked by default**. They will only be activated if you provide explicit, active consent (Strict Opt-In) via the cookie consent banner, in strict compliance with Israel's Privacy Law (Amendment 13) and GDPR guidelines.

**Essential/Technical Cookies:**
The site utilizes minimal local storage (LocalStorage) and cookies strictly necessary for its core technical functionality. This includes storing your core language preference (`lang` cookie) and recording your technical choice to accept or decline analytics (`cookie-consent`). These essential functional elements do not track you, are not used for marketing, and do not require prior consent.

## 4. Information Security & Data Retention (SLA)
We implement advanced technical and organizational measures to protect your information from unauthorized access. In accordance with the data minimization principle which requires deleting information that is no longer needed, "Contact Us" inquiries from the website are routed directly to the company email. If they have not matured into active business engagements, a system rule automatically and permanently deletes them within 60 days. You may demand an immediate manual deletion ("Right to be Forgotten") earlier, and it will be processed within 30 days as required by law.

## 5. Your Rights (Review, Correction, and Deletion)
Under the Privacy Protection Law and Amendment 13, you are entitled to:
* Review your personal information stored with us.
* Demand its correction if it is inaccurate or outdated.
* Demand its deletion ("Right to be Forgotten") at any time if it is no longer required for legitimate purposes.

## 6. Contact Us
For any request to exercise your rights (including deletion requests), you can contact us directly by email: [Office@fractal.co.il](mailto:Office@fractal.co.il). We will handle your request within the timeframe stipulated by law.
